About

I am French and British, grew up in Asia, and study cell biology at University College London (UCL).

Interests:
  • Biology:
    - Ageing: understanding ageing today, when human lifespans are increasing, could help us ward off age-related diseases such as cancer or Parkinson's disease. Solving ageing is an exciting prospect! Ageing is a bulging Gordian knot made of many different "tangled strings" (microbiology, genetics, cell biology, etc.). Once undone and tied together, this newly formed Ariadne thread bridging these fields would offer us a big-picture solution of the microscopic bustle in our bodies. This solution would have a colossal impact on scientific, economic, political, cultural, and social areas, affecting everything that we know of today.
